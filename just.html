About NariRaksha

NariRaksha is dedicated to ensuring women's safety by providing real-time location tracking, instant emergency alerts, and one-click communication tools for women traveling alone, especially during unusual hours. Our platform connects women to the nearest police station and their designated guardians, ensuring help is always within reach.

With just a single click, women can activate an emergency buzzer, make a quick call, and send a distress message to alert authorities and family members. NariRaksha is a trusted companion for women, offering peace of mind and a safer journey, no matter the time or place.

Our vision is a world where women can move freely and fearlessly, knowing that support is always just one click away.
